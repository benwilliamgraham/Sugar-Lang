Sugar is a simple language that compiles into C++. It takes the increadible speed and control of C/C++ and puts it into the body of a scripting language.

The goal is to be able to prototype robust code quickly and safely, and then requiring little effort to take it to production, vastly shortening the development process and providing a much nicer experience.
